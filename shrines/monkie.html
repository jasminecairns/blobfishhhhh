Why I love it so much

The first thing that drew me into the show was the animation. The characters in Monkie Kid may move differently from what you would expect from other Lego movies and shows, like Ninjago or the Lego Movie. Thanks to the power of 2D animation, everyone in the show is free to squash and and stretch however the artists see fit. I absolutely love how expressive the characters are!

Also, the writing gets better with each season*! In the first season, the characters and story are simple but charming. But as the show progress, more depth and little details are added to the characters, and each arc becomes more intense! I adored seeing the characters grow and overcome new challenges (rewrite). Also, the character designs are so sick too! Who knew Legos could look so cool???

The aspect of Lego Monkie Kid that resonates with me the most is the culture behind it. In the Lego documentary,A LEGO Journey to the East, designers traveled to China to study its culture, especially The Journey to the West and Monkey King (would recommend checking it if you can btw). The writing team consistently checked with Chinese Lego employees to ensure that not only the story and dialogue are appropriate in the context of Chinese culture, but also that their culture oozed through the theme and show's art. You can tell from the character designs, backgrounds, music, and references to Journey to the West. I really love how a Lego show can portray Asian culture in a way that's so cool and modern, while also acknowledging and respecting their roots. Literally so peak and kinda inspiring. Also I loved the Lunar New Year special so much !!!! It reminds me of when I've celebrated it too ^^

Oh yea the backgrounds are super pretty! Love their use of color and how they can easily switch from modern and ancient settings!
